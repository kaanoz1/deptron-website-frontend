Double First Place for YTU Students at "Europe's Technofest"

Yildiz Technical University students Meryem Koc and her friends made Turkey proud by winning two major awards at Shift Appens, one of Europe's prestigious technology events.

According to Ekonomim.com; At the Shift Appens hackathon held in Portugal and closely followed by the technology world, the Deptron Robotics team left its mark on the night with the innovative software they developed.

Known as "Europe's Technofest"

In the competition where young talents push the boundaries and which is described as "Europe's Technofest", our team won the appreciation of both the technical jury and the participants with the AI-supported autonomous systems they developed. The Deptron team reached the top in two critical categories after a challenging 48-hour marathon.

"Yildiz Technical University students once again demonstrated the power of Turkish engineering in the European arena. This double victory is proof that we will have a say in the technologies of the future."

- Ekonomim News

Achievements Won

At the end of the competition, the Deptron Robotics team took home the following awards:

  • 🏆 1st Place Award (Best Solution): Best project in technical excellence and innovation category.
  • 🌟 Public Favorite: The most liked and voted project award.

Outlook for the Future

This success achieved under the leadership of our founder Meryem Koc has been an important source of motivation for Deptron Robotics to reach its goals in the global market. The team aims to move Turkey to the top ranks in the autonomous systems league by taking the technologies they have developed even further.
