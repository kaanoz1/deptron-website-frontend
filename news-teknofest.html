OUR ACHIEVEMENTS / TEKNOFEST 📅 September 2024

Digital Technologies in Industry Finalist: Deptron Robotics

We achieved great success by reaching the finals with our domestic autonomous warehouse robot in the "Digital Technologies in Industry Competition" organized within the scope of TEKNOFEST, the world's largest aviation, space and technology festival.

As Deptron Robotics, our project received full marks from the jury and visitors at Teknofest, which we attended with the vision of reducing foreign dependency in industrial logistics and strengthening domestic technology. During the competition process, we had the opportunity to demonstrate the capabilities of our AI-supported autonomous mobile robot (AMR), which optimizes warehouse management processes, in the field.

Why Digital Technologies in Industry?

This competition aims to support solutions that will accelerate the digital transformation of factories and warehouses. As Deptron, we put our name among the finalists with the following features we offer:

  • Full Autonomous Driving: Precise navigation in GPS-free environments with ROS2 infrastructure and SLAM technology.
  • High Efficiency: Smart route planning providing up to 35% efficiency increase in warehouse operations.
  • Safety Standards: Sensor hardware minimizing work accidents and compliance with ISO standards.
"Our goal is to rapidly scale sales and become a global brand by earning foreign currency for our country."

- Deptron Team

Team Spirit and Future

Our core team consisting of Meryem Koc, Serhat Saygi, Gulsah Keskin, Recep Dogan and Burak Oflaz accelerated their product development processes with the experience gained during the Teknofest process. This finalist achievement is just a beginning; combined with our successes in Europe, it is a stepping stone that will carry us to the global market.
