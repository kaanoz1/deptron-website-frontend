Meet Nova V1: The Warehouse Assistant of the Future

Designed to minimize time losses and safety risks caused by manual labor in warehouse logistics, Nova V1 offers an autonomous revolution at industrial standards.

Why Nova V1?

The Nova series we developed as Deptron Robotics is not just a machine that carries loads, it is the intelligence of your warehouse. Unlike traditional forklifts and manual transport methods, Nova V1 aims to reduce operational errors to zero by working completely autonomously.

Technical Superiorities

  • High Payload Capacity: Thanks to its modular structure, it offers a payload capacity from 0 to 1,500 kg, adapting to heavy industry conditions.
  • Voice Assistant Support: Its biggest feature distinguishing it from competitors is its integrated voice assistant. Operators can assign tasks and query status by speaking directly to the robot.
  • Mecanum Wheel Technology: It provides maximum maneuverability with the ability to turn on the spot and crab walk even in narrow corridors.
  • ROS 2 & SLAM: It performs centimeter-level mapping in indoor areas without GPS signals with advanced Lidar sensors and SLAM algorithms.

Contributions to Your Business

With the integration of Nova V1, up to 35% efficiency increase is achieved in your facility. While work accidents and safety risks are minimized, it makes stock tracking instantaneous by working fully integrated with ERP/WMS systems.

"Developed with domestic production power and global technology vision, Nova reduces warehouse costs while increasing operational speed."
